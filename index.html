About Us

TPUSA at UC San Diego is dedicated to fostering a community of innovative and forward-thinking conservative students. We strive to empower our members to lead, advocate, and create a vibrant environment for dialogue and change on campus.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build a network of like-minded individuals who are passionate about promoting free enterprise, individual liberty, and limited government. We aim to inspire leadership through educational events, meaningful discussions, and hands-on experiences.
